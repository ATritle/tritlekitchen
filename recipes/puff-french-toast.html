Puff French Toast
Grandma Peggy

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs - room temperature
  • 2 1/2 TBSP sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • dash cinnamon
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • avocado or vegetable oil Not cooking spray
  • thick-sliced bread
  • maple syrup or cinnamon sugar - for topping

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, sugar, salt, vanilla, cinnamon, and milk until well combined.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the flour and baking powder.
  3. Slowly add the flour mixture to the egg mixture and whisk until smooth.
  4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  5. Dip both sides of the bread into the batter, then place in the heated skillet. Cook for 2–3 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with maple syrup or cinnamon sugar.
