Peanut Butter Cups
eatingbirdfood.com

Ingredients:

    Chocolate layer:
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 TBSP coconut oil - melted
    • Peanut butter layer:
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 TBSP coconut oil - melted
  • pinch of sea salt - optional

Directions:

  1. Line mini muffin tins with paper or silicone liners.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the chocolate chips and coconut oil in 30-second intervals, stirring after each interval until smooth.
  3. Spoon 1–2 teaspoons of chocolate into the bottom of each muffin liner.
  4. Place the muffin tin in the freezer for 5–10 minutes to allow the chocolate to set.
  5. In another microwave-safe bowl, combine the peanut butter, honey, coconut oil, and salt (if using). Microwave for 15–20 seconds until slightly melted and pourable, stirring if needed.
  6. Remove the muffin tin from the freezer and spoon 1–2 teaspoons of the peanut butter mixture over the chocolate. Return the tin to the freezer for 5–10 minutes.
  7. Remove the tin from the freezer and spoon the remaining chocolate over the peanut butter layer. Sprinkle with sea salt, if desired.
  8. Return the muffin tin to the freezer for 30–60 minutes, or until fully set.
  9. Store in the refrigerator for 5–7 days or in the freezer for up to 2 months.
