Chocolate Pudding Shots

Ingredients:

  • 3.9 oz package chocolate pudding
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup cake vodka
  • 1/2 cup Irish cream
  • 8 oz Cool Whip - thawed
  • 2 oz plastic cups with lids

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk the pudding mix and milk together until smooth. Let stand for 5 minutes to thicken.
  2. Stir in the vodka and Irish cream until well combined.
  3. Fold in the whipped topping until evenly incorporated.
  4. Fill 2-ounce cups, place lids on, and freeze until ready to serve.
  5. Optional: Let sit at room temperature for a few minutes before serving if frozen solid.
