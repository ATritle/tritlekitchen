Caramel Corn
Old Dutch

Ingredients:

  • 1 package Old Dutch Puffcorn
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar - packed
  • 2/3 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Optional: chocolate chips - melted

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°F.
  2. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine the butter, brown sugar, and light corn syrup. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the mixture is fully melted and smooth.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the baking soda. The mixture will foam—this is normal.
  4. Place the Old Dutch Puffcorn in a large bowl. Pour the caramel sauce over the puffcorn and gently stir until evenly coated.
  5. Spread the mixture onto a large baking sheet and bake for 45 minutes, stirring every 10–15 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and spread onto wax paper to cool. Once cooled, break into pieces.
  7. Optional: Drizzle melted chocolate chips over the puffcorn after baking and allow to set before serving.
