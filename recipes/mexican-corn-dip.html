Mexican Corn Dip - Tammy Kuhlmann

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mayo
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 2 - 11oz or 15.25oz cans Mexican or Southwest sweet corn - drained
  • 10 oz can Rotel
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 green onions - sliced
  • 1 bunch of cilantro - chopped

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the mayonnaise, sour cream, corn, and Rotel. Mix until well blended.
  2. Fold in the cheddar cheese, green onions, and cilantro until evenly incorporated.
