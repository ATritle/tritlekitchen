Korean Beef
slowcookermeals.com

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 LBS flank steak - cut into 1/4" strips
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1 & 1/2 TBSP sesame oil
  • 2 tsp garlic - minced
  • 1/2 tsp fresh ginger - grated
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 1/2 cup liquid aminos
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 3-4 green onions - chopped

Directions:

  1. Place the sliced flank steak and cornstarch in a large resealable plastic bag. Seal and shake until the steak is evenly coated. Transfer to the bottom of a 6-quart crockpot.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the sesame oil, garlic, fresh ginger, rice vinegar, liquid aminos, beef broth, brown sugar, and red pepper flakes until well combined. Pour the mixture over the flank steak.
  3. Cover and cook on Low for 4–8 hours, or until the steak is tender.
  4. Serve over rice and garnish with sliced green onions.
