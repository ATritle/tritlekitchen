Chunky Blonde Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter - softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar - packed
  • 2 eggs - room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups flour - sifted
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup white chocolate or vanilla chips
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 1 cup macadamia nuts

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar with a hand mixer until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture and mix until just combined.
  5. Fold in the white chocolate chips and nuts, then spread the batter evenly into a greased 9x13-inch pan.
  6. Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
