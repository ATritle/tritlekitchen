Dutch Baby - Regular

Ingredients:

  • 2 TBSP unsalted butter
  • 3 XL eggs - room temp
  • 1/2 cup + 1/2 TBSP milk
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free flour
  • pinch salt
  • splash vanilla extract
  • dash cinnamon
  • dash nutmeg

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Place butter in a 10-inch cast iron skillet and put it in the oven to melt.
  3. Whisk eggs and milk together in a bowl. Do not use a blender.
  4. Whisk in flour until smooth and slightly thickened, about the consistency of pourable cake batter.
  5. Carefully remove the skillet from the oven and swirl the melted butter to evenly coat the pan.
  6. Pour the batter into the skillet and bake for 15 minutes. Rotate the pan and bake for an additional 15 minutes, until puffed and golden.
