Teriyaki Chicken Crispy Rice
TikTok - @kalejunkie

Ingredients:

  • Crispy rice:
  • 2 cups jasmine rice - rinsed and cooked
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1 TBSP toasted sesame oil
  • 2 TBSP teriyaki sauce - G Hughes
  • Chicken:
  • 1 rotisserie chicken or 1-2 LBS chicken - cooked and diced
  • 1/4 cup teriyaki sauce
  • Salad:
  • 1/2 cup English cucumber - sliced and cut into quarters
  • 1/2 cup red cabbage - finely sliced
  • 1/2 cup cilantro - chopped
  • 1 bunch green onions - finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup roasted unsalted peanuts
  • Optional: 1 red bell pepper - diced small
  • Creamy sesame ginger dressing:
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 TBSP toasted sesame seed oil
  • 3 TBSP liquid aminos
  • 3 TBSP rice vinegar
  • 2 TBSP maple syrup
  • 2 TBSP nut butter - peanut, almond, or cashew
  • 2-3 cloves garlic - mashed
  • 1 TBSP fresh ginger - grated

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Rinse the rice under cold water 3–4 times, or until the water runs mostly clear. This helps remove excess starch and prevents stickiness.
  2. In a medium saucepan over medium-high heat, combine the rinsed rice and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to the lowest setting. Cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 12–15 minutes without lifting the lid.
  3. Remove from heat and let sit, covered, for 15–20 minutes.
  4. Remove the lid and fluff with a fork. Allow the rice to cool completely, then transfer to a bowl and toss with toasted sesame oil and teriyaki sauce until well coated.
  5. Spread the rice evenly onto the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 15 minutes, stir, then bake for an additional 15 minutes. Continue baking, stirring every 10–15 minutes as needed, until the rice reaches your desired crispiness.
  6. If using a rotisserie chicken, remove the meat from the bones, chop, and toss with teriyaki sauce. If using uncooked chicken, cook in a skillet until fully cooked, then coat with teriyaki sauce.
  7. Add all dressing ingredients to a small blender and blend for about 1 minute, or until smooth.
  8. Chop the cucumber, cabbage, cilantro, green onions, and peanuts. Place in a large bowl.
  9. Add the chicken and crispy rice to the bowl and toss well. Drizzle with the desired amount of dressing and serve. Store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
