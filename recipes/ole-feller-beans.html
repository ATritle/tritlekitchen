Ole Feller Beans

Ingredients:

  • 1 LBS ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup onion - diced
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar - packed
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 LBS bacon - diced
  • 2 TBSP white corn syrup
  • 15 oz can kidney beans - rinsed and drained
  • 15 oz can butter beans - rinsed and drained
  • 15 oz can pork and beans
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • 2 TBSP mustard
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, brown the beef and bacon until fully cooked. Drain excess grease if needed.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and stir to combine.
  3. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Alternatively, cook in a crockpot on Low for 6–8 hours.
