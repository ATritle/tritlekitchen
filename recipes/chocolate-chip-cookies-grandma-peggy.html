Chocolate Chip Cookies
Grandma Peggy

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups butter - softened
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups brown sugar - packed
  • 2 eggs - room temperature
  • 1 TBSP vanilla extract
  • 4 1/2 cups flour - sifted
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs and vanilla extract. Mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the butter mixture and mix until just combined.
  6. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
