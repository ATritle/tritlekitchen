Caramel Popcorn Balls

Ingredients:

  • 14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 cup brown sugar - packed
  • 3/4 cup light corn syrup
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 16 cups popped popcorn
  • Optional:M&M's, nuts, sea salt, or melted chocolate - drizzle

Directions:

  1. In a large saucepan, melt the butter, brown sugar, and corn syrup. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  2. Once boiling, reduce the heat to medium and add the sweetened condensed milk. Cook for 4–5 minutes, stirring frequently and scraping the bottom of the pan to prevent burning. Cook for about 4 minutes for a softer caramel.
  3. Place the popcorn in a large bowl. Pour the caramel mixture over the popcorn and stir until evenly coated.
  4. Lightly spray your hands with cooking spray and shape the popcorn into balls once it has cooled enough to handle.
  5. Optional: Once the caramel popcorn has cooled slightly, mix in M&M’s, chopped nuts, or a sprinkle of sea salt before forming the popcorn balls. You can also drizzle melted chocolate over the finished popcorn balls.
