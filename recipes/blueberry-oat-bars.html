Blueberry Oat Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups rolled oats - divided
  • 1 cup almonds or almond butter
  • 2 scoops vanilla protein powder
  • 10 dates - pitted & chopped
  • 1/2 cup agave or maple syrup
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 cup blueberries

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a food processor, combine 1 cup oats, almonds, protein powder, and salt. Process until finely ground.
  3. Add the dates and process in 30-second increments until well combined. Do not overmix, as the mixture can become gummy.
  4. If using frozen blueberries, warm them slightly in the microwave. In a bowl, combine the blueberries with the remaining 1 cup oats, vanilla, and agave.
  5. Line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper. Press the mixture firmly and evenly into the pan.
  6. Bake for 20–25 minutes, or until slightly golden.
  7. Allow to cool completely before cutting into bars.
  8. Store in the freezer for best texture.
