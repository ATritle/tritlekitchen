Dry Brine

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup kosher salt
  • 1 TBSP light brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter - room temp
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 whole turkey or chicken

Directions:

  1. Rinse the chicken under cold water and pat dry thoroughly inside and out.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the salt, sugar, and pepper. Rub the mixture all over the chicken, including inside the cavity and under the skin of the breasts.
  3. Place the chicken on a baking sheet and refrigerate uncovered overnight, or for at least 8–12 hours.
  4. Preheat the oven to 500°F and position the rack in the lowest setting.
  5. Rinse off the brine under cold water and pat dry again inside and out.
  6. Place the chicken breast-side up on a rack in a roasting pan. Let sit at room temperature for 30–60 minutes.
  7. Rub softened butter all over the chicken, including under the skin of the breasts.
  8. Tie the legs together with kitchen twine. Secure the twine around the body near the neck opening and tuck the wings underneath.
  9. Add 1 cup water to the roasting pan. Roast for 30 minutes at 500°F, then reduce the oven temperature to 350°F and continue roasting for 1½ to 2 hours.
  10. Rotate the pan after 1 hour. Roast until a thermometer inserted into the thickest part of the thigh registers 155°F.
  11. Remove from the oven and let rest for 20 minutes before transferring to a cutting board to carve and serve.
