Vegan Zucchini Muffins
minimalistbaker.com

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup avocado oil
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 2/3 cup coconut sugar
  • 2/3 cup maple or agave syrup
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups zucchini - shredded and squeezed
  • 3 cups almond flour
  • 1 1/3 cups + 1/2 cup flour - sifted
  • 4 TBSP flaxseed meal
  • 5 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 TBSP cinnamon
  • 1 tsp sea salt

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and grease or line a mini muffin tin, regular muffin tin, mini bread pans, or a regular bread pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the avocado oil, almond milk, coconut sugar, syrup, and vanilla until well combined.
  3. Fold in the zucchini until well coated. Stir in the almond flour, flour, flaxseed meal, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and sea salt until fully incorporated.
  4. Bake mini muffins for 15–20 minutes, regular muffins for 25–30 minutes, mini bread pans for 40–45 minutes, or a regular bread pan for about 1 hour, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
