Banana Greek Yogurt Muffins

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas - mashed
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 eggs - room temp
  • 2 cups rolled quick oats
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1½ tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Lightly spray a muffin tin with cooking spray or line with paper liners.
  3. In a food processor, combine the bananas, Greek yogurt, eggs, quick oats, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Blend until the oats are fully broken down and the batter is smooth and creamy.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  5. Divide the batter evenly among the muffin cups, filling each about ¾ full. Sprinkle additional chocolate chips on top, if desired.
  6. Bake for 15–20 minutes, or until the tops are set and a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  7. Store in an airtight container for up to 1 week.
