Apple Cider Honey Whiskey

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups apple cider
  • 2 cups honey whiskey
  • 3 cinnamon sticks
  • 2 lemons - juiced
  • 1-2 TBSP honey
  • 2 apples - sliced thick

Directions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a crockpot and cook on Low for 2–3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  2. Taste and adjust the honey to reach your desired level of sweetness.
  3. Serve warm and garnish with apples.
