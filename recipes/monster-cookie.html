Monster Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs - room temperature
  • 1 1/4 cups light brown sugar - packed
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 12 oz jar creamy peanut butter
  • 1 stick unsalted butter - softened
  • 4 1/2 cups old-fashioned oats
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup M&M's

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, beat the eggs, light brown sugar, and granulated sugar until well combined.
  3. Add the salt, vanilla, peanut butter, and butter. Mix until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add the oats to the bowl. Sprinkle the baking soda evenly over the oats, then stir until fully incorporated.
  5. Fold in the M&M’s. Scoop the dough into balls.
  6. Place 12 dough balls onto each baking sheet and bake for 8–10 minutes, or until lightly golden brown.
