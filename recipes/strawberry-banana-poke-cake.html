Strawberry Banana Poke Cake
Tammy Kuhlmann

Ingredients:

  • 21.50 oz package all butter pound cake - cubed
  • 3 oz package strawberry Jell-o
  • 1 large container strawberries - sliced
  • 4-5 bananas - sliced
  • 3.4 oz package vanilla pudding
  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 8 oz Cool Whip - thawed

Directions:

  1. In a 9x13-inch pan, arrange the cubed pound cake evenly across the bottom.
  2. In a medium saucepan, bring 1 cup of water to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium and stir in the gelatin (Jell-O) for about 2 minutes.
  3. Once fully dissolved, add 1 cup of cold water to the mixture and pour it evenly over the pound cake.
  4. Layer the sliced strawberries and bananas over the gelatin and pound cake.
  5. In a medium bowl, whisk the vanilla pudding mix and milk for about 2 minutes. Refrigerate for 5 minutes to allow it to set.
  6. Once the pudding has thickened, fold in the whipped topping and spread evenly over the cake.
