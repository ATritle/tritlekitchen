Pumpkin Pie Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 TBSP maple syrup
  • pinch of salt - optional

Directions:

  1. Add the cottage cheese to a blender and blend for 30–60 seconds until completely smooth.
  2. Add the pumpkin puree, vanilla extract, pumpkin pie spice, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt (optional). Blend again until smooth and creamy.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness with additional maple syrup if desired.
