Thai Chicken Lettuce Wrap
thebusybaker.ca

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic - minced
  • 1 Large onion - diced
  • 1 cup carrot - shredded
  • 1 LBS chicken - ground or cubed
  • 3 TBSP liquid aminos
  • 2 tsp rice vinegar
  • 2 TBSP brown sugar
  • 1 TBSP natural peanut butter
  • 1 tsp chili paste
  • 2 tsp cornstarch
  • 2 tsp water
  • 12 leaves romaine or butter lettuce
  • 1/4 cup peanuts - chopped
  • 3-4 green onions - chopped

Directions:

  1. Add the ground chicken to a large skillet over medium heat and cook until browned and fully cooked through.
  2. Stir in the liquid aminos, rice vinegar, brown sugar, peanut butter, and chili paste. Mix until well combined.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the cornstarch and water until fully dissolved.
  4. Pour the cornstarch mixture into the skillet and stir continuously as the sauce thickens.
  5. Remove from heat and spoon the chicken mixture into lettuce leaves. Garnish with chopped peanuts and sliced green onions before serving.
