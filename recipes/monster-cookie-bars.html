Monster Cookie Bars - Jocelyn MHS

Ingredients:

  • 1 stick unsalted butter - softened
  • 1 cup light brown sugar - packed
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs - room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups creamy peanut butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 4 1/2 cups quick oats
  • 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet mini chocolate chips
  • 1 cup M&M's

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Lightly grease or line a 12x18-inch baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the eggs, peanut butter, and vanilla. Mix until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the baking soda and quick oats. Stir into the wet mixture until fully incorporated.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips and M&M’s until evenly distributed.
  6. Drop dollops of dough evenly across the baking sheet rather than placing it all in the center. Gently press the dough into an even layer.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes. Do not overbake.
