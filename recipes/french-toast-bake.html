French Toast Bake

Ingredients:

  • 18 slices thick bread - cubed
  • 12 eggs
  • 12-16 oz cream cheese - cubed
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1-2 tsp cinnamon & sugar
  • 1-2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, maple syrup, and cinnamon sugar until well combined. Set aside.
  2. Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer half of the cubed bread in the dish, followed by half of the cubed cream cheese. Repeat with the remaining bread and cream cheese.
  3. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the bread. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  4. Remove from the refrigerator 30 minutes before baking, keeping the dish covered.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Bake covered for 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for an additional 20–25 minutes, or until set and lightly golden.
  6. Serve warm with maple syrup.
