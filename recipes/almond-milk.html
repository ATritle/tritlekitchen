Almond Milk

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups raw organic almonds
  • filtered water
  • 3 TBSP stevia
  • 1 TBSP white Dominican vanilla
  • Optional:
  • vanilla bean paste
  • honey
  • cinnamon
  • cardamom
  • cocoa

Directions:

  1. Soak the almonds in filtered water for 10–12 hours.
  2. Drain and rinse thoroughly with cold water.
  3. Blend 1 cup soaked almonds with 2 cups filtered water until the mixture is as smooth as possible.
  4. Strain the mixture through a nut milk bag, squeezing out as much liquid as possible into a bowl or jar.
  5. Do not discard the leftover pulp. Reserve it for homemade almond flour (see almond flour recipe).
  6. For a sweeter milk, stir in stevia and vanilla to taste.
  7. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 7–10 days. Shake well before each use.
