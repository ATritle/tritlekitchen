Tortellini Soup
TikTok - @heresyourbite

Ingredients:

  • 1 TBSP unsalted butter
  • 1 TBSP olive oil
  • 1 medium onion - diced
  • 1/2 cup celery - finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup carrots - finely chopped
  • 6-8 cloves garlic - minced
  • 1-2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • salt & pepper
  • 1 LBS ground Italian sausage
  • 3 TBSP tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 28 oz diced tomatoes - drained
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 Parmesan rind
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 18 oz package four cheese tortelloni pasta
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

Directions:

  1. In a Dutch oven over medium heat, add the olive oil, butter, onions, celery, carrots, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Sauté for 5–7 minutes, until fragrant and vegetables begin to soften.
  2. Add the Italian sausage to the same pot and cook until browned. Create a space in the center of the pan and add the tomato paste. Let the tomato paste cook for about 1 minute before stirring it into the sausage mixture.
  3. Deglaze the pan with white wine and cook for about 2 minutes. Add the tomatoes, chicken stock, bay leaf, and Parmesan rind. Simmer uncovered for about 30 minutes.
  4. Remove the bay leaf and Parmesan rind. Add the spinach and tortellini and cook for 3–4 minutes, then stir in the heavy cream.
