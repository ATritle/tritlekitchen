Dunkaroo Dip

Ingredients:

  • 1 box Funfetti cake mix
  • 12 oz vanilla yogurt
  • 8 oz Cool Whip
  • sprinkles

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the vanilla yogurt and whipped topping. Mix until smooth and well blended.
  2. Stir in the funfetti cake mix and sprinkles until evenly incorporated.
  3. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
  4. Serve with graham crackers.
