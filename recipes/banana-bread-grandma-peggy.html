Banana Bread
Grandma Peggy

Ingredients:

  • 3/4 cup butter - softened
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3-4 blackened bananas - mashed
  • 2 eggs - room temp
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cup flour - sifted
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk or sour milk - (1/2 cup milk and 1 TBSP vinegar)
  • Optional:
  • chocolate chips
  • walnuts

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the bananas, eggs, and vanilla. Mix just until combined—do not overmix.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture, alternating with the buttermilk, and mix until just combined.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips or walnuts, if desired.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and bake for 1 to 1½ hours, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. If dividing the batter between two pans, bake for about 1 hour.
