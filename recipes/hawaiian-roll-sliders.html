Hawaiian Roll Sliders

Ingredients:

  • 2 TBSP olive oil
  • 1 large onion - thinly sliced
  • salt - to taste
  • 4 TBSP unsalted butter - melted
  • 1 tsp poppy seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic - minced
  • 12 Hawaiian rolls - sliced in half
  • 2 LBS ham - sliced thinly
  • 1 LBS sliced Swiss cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, add the olive oil, onion, and salt. Cook until the onions are caramelized to your desired color.
  3. In a small bowl, combine the melted butter, poppy seeds, garlic, and Worcestershire sauce.
  4. Place the bottom halves of the Hawaiian rolls in the prepared pan. Layer with ham slices, caramelized onions, and cheese. Add the top halves of the rolls, then brush the poppy seed mixture evenly over the tops.
  5. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the tops are lightly golden.
  6. Serve with the honey mustard dipping sauce (see recipe).
