Potato Salad
Lisa Smith

Ingredients:

  • 8-10 eggs - cooked, peeled, chopped
  • 4 LBS potatoes - peeled and diced
  • 1 medium onion - finely chopped
  • 1/2 TBSP salt
  • 1/2 TBSP black pepper
  • 2 cups mayo (not Miracle Whip)
  • 3 TBSP mustard
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Directions:

  1. In a large pot, add the diced potatoes and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil and cook until the potatoes are about ¾ cooked.
  2. Drain the water and run cold water over the potatoes to stop the cooking process.
  3. Allow the potatoes to cool slightly.
  4. Optional: While the potatoes are still warm, sprinkle with a small amount of vinegar and toss gently. This helps the potatoes absorb additional flavor.
  5. In a medium bowl, combine the onion, salt, pepper, mayonnaise, mustard, vinegar, sugar, and eggs. Mix well, then refrigerate the mixture to allow the flavors to develop.
  6. Place the potatoes in a large bowl and break them apart with a fork to the desired size.
  7. Add the mayonnaise mixture to the potatoes and mix well. Refrigerate for 2–3 hours before serving.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste before serving.
