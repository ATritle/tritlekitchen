Egg Roll in a Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 LBS ground chicken, turkey, beef, or pork
  • 2 bags coleslaw mix
  • 2 TBSP liquid aminos
  • 1 TBSP ground ginger
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 4-5 green onions - sliced
  • Optional: 1-2 tsp red pepper flakes

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the sausage, stirring and crumbling until fully cooked through. Drain excess grease if necessary.
  2. Stir in the garlic powder, ground ginger, and liquid aminos until well combined.
  3. Add the coleslaw mix and cook until wilted to your desired tenderness.
  4. Note: Do not add additional salt, as the liquid aminos provide sufficient seasoning.
