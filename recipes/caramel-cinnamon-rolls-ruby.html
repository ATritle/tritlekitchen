Caramel Cinnamon Rolls - Ruby

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup Crisco
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs - room temperature, beaten
  • 1 1/4 tsp yeast
  • 6 cups flour - sifted
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 3-4 TBSP butter - melted
  • cinnamon & sugar
  • Caramel sauce:
  • 2 cups brown sugar - packed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup butter - softened
  • 4 TBSP white Karo syrup
  • Optional: pecan or walnuts

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the boiling water, sugar, Crisco, and salt. Stir until dissolved and allow the mixture to cool to lukewarm.
  2. Add the eggs and yeast. Stir in the flour gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Transfer the dough to a large greased bowl, cover loosely, and let rise until doubled in size (about 2–3 hours).
  4. Punch down the dough, cover with plastic wrap, and let rest for another 2–3 hours.
  5. Divide the dough in half. On a lightly floured surface, roll each portion into a large rectangle.
  6. Brush with melted butter and sprinkle evenly with cinnamon and sugar.
  7. Roll tightly into logs and cut each into 12 rolls. Place the rolls into two greased 9x13-inch pans.
  8. In a saucepan, melt the brown sugar, water, and butter over medium heat, stirring until smooth. Stir in the Karo syrup.
  9. Pour half of the caramel mixture into each pan and sprinkle with nuts, if desired.
  10. Cover loosely and let rise overnight at room temperature.
  11. In the morning, preheat the oven to 350°F and bake for 15–20 minutes, or until golden brown.
  12. Immediately invert the hot rolls onto a baking sheet or serving tray to allow the caramel to coat the tops. (This step is not necessary if frosting instead.)
  13. Cover loosely with foil or a clean kitchen towel to keep warm before serving.
