Garlic Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1-2 LBS chicken - diced
  • 5 cloves garlic - minced
  • 1/2 cup liquid aminos
  • 2 TBSP toasted sesame oil
  • 4 TBSP honey
  • 1 TBSP cornstarch
  • 4-6 green onions - sliced
  • 1 can water chestnuts
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli
  • 1 bag frozen cauliflower rice
  • 1/4-1/2 bag frozen peas and carrots
  • 2 eggs
  • salt & pepper - to taste
  • sesame seeds - garnish

Directions:

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the chicken with salt and pepper until fully cooked through.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the minced garlic, liquid aminos, sesame oil, honey, and cornstarch.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and pour the garlic sauce over the chicken. Stir to coat, then add the water chestnuts and broccoli. Cook until the vegetables are tender.
  4. In a separate skillet over medium heat, cook the cauliflower rice with the peas and carrots, seasoning with salt and pepper. Once heated through, push the rice to one side, add the eggs, scramble until cooked, then mix everything together.
  5. Note: If using xanthan gum in the garlic sauce, start with a very small amount. Stir well and add more gradually if needed, as the sauce will thicken quickly.
  6. Combine the garlic chicken mixture with the cauliflower rice. Garnish with sesame seeds and sliced green onions before serving.
