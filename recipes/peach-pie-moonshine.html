Peach Pie Moonshine

Ingredients:

  • 128 oz Welch's white grape peach juice
  • 15 oz can sliced peaches - heavy syrup
  • 15 oz can sugar-free sliced peaches
  • 6-7 cinnamon sticks
  • 750 ml peach schnapps
  • 375 ml Everclear
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar - packed

Directions:

  1. In a large stockpot, combine the juice, peaches, sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon sticks. Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer for 30–60 minutes.
  3. Remove the cinnamon sticks and allow the mixture to cool to room temperature.
  4. Stir in the peach schnapps and Everclear.
  5. Strain the mixture through cheesecloth to remove small pieces of peaches.
  6. Ladle into jars and add 2–3 peach slices to each jar.
  7. Yields approximately 12–14 pint mason jars.
