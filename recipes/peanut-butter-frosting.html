Peanut Butter Frosting

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup chunky or creamy peanut butter
  • 3 TBSP butter - softened
  • 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, cream the butter and peanut butter together. Gradually add the powdered sugar and mix until smooth.
  2. Stir in the milk and vanilla until well combined.
  3. If the frosting is too thick, add 1 tablespoon milk at a time. If it is too thin, add additional powdered sugar until the desired consistency is reached.
