Elote Chicken Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 LBS chicken - cooked & diced
  • 4-6 TBSP taco seasoning
  • 12-16 oz package fusilli pasta
  • 1 medium red onion - diced
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro - chopped
  • 2 - 15 oz cans sweet corn - roasted
  • 1 cup Cotija cheese
  • SAUCE:
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup mayo
  • Tajín - to taste
  • 1 fresh lime - zested and juiced
  • drizzle honey - to taste
  • 1-2 TBSP taco seasoning

Directions:

  1. Cook the chicken using one of the following methods:
    • Grill: Cook over medium heat until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
    • Bake: Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
    • Skillet: Cook over medium-high heat for 15–20 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through.
  2. While the chicken is cooking, bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook the pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet over medium-low heat, roast the sweet corn, stirring occasionally to prevent burning.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the sour cream, mayonnaise, taco seasoning, Tajín, lime zest, lime juice, and honey (to taste). Mix until smooth.
  5. Add the red onion, cilantro, cooked chicken, cooked pasta, roasted corn, and Cotija cheese. Toss until evenly combined.
