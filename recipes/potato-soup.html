Potato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 3 large russet potatoes - peeled & diced
  • 1/2 onion - diced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  • to taste salt & pepper
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp paprika
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 LBS bacon - cooked & crumbled

Directions:

  1. In a large pot over medium-high heat, cook the bacon until crispy. Remove from the pot and crumble once cooled.
  2. Reserve 1 tablespoon of bacon grease in the pot. Add the onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the diced potatoes, chicken broth, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 12–15 minutes, or until the potatoes are fork-tender.
  4. Reduce heat to low and stir in the milk, heavy cream, cheddar cheese, and sour cream. Cook until the cheese is fully melted and the soup is smooth.
  5. Stir in the crumbled bacon and serve warm.
