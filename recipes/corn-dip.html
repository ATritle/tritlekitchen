Corn Dip - Jesi Fuller

Ingredients:

  • 4 - 11 oz white shoepeg corn - drained
  • 11 oz jar jalapenos - chopped reserve some juice
  • 1 small red onion - diced
  • 2 cups mayo
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • dash of garlic powder

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the corn, jalapeños, onion, mayo, cheddar cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic. Mix until well blended.
