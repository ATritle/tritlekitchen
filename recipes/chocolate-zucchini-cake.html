Chocolate Zucchini Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour - sifted
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened natural cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup olive or avocado oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar - packed
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup sour cream or plain vanilla yogurt
  • 4 eggs - room temperature
  • 3 cups zucchini - shredded and lightly squeezed
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish or a muffin tin.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, using a hand mixer or a stand mixer fitted with the whisk attachment, beat the oil, sugar, brown sugar, eggs, vanilla, sour cream, and zucchini until well combined.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and beat on medium speed until fully incorporated.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Note: The batter will be slightly thick.
  7. If using a 9x13-inch pan, bake for about 45 minutes. If using a muffin tin, bake for 18–22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool completely before frosting.
  9. See the chocolate buttercream frosting recipe for topping.
  10. Cover and store in the refrigerator for up to 5 days.
