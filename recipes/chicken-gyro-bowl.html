Chicken Gyro Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz chicken
  • 1/2 red onion - diced
  • 1 each English cucumber - diced
  • 1-2 Roma tomatoes - diced
  • 3-4 Kalamata olives - diced
  • 8 oz feta cheese
  • 1 cup tzatziki sauce - see tzatziki sauce receipe
  • MARINADE:
  • 2 cloves garlic - finely minced
  • 1 TBSP red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 lemon - juiced
  • 2 TBSP extra-virgin oil
  • 3 TBSP greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • 3/4 tsp salt

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine all marinade ingredients and whisk until well blended.
  2. Place the chicken in a large resealable plastic bag. Pour the marinade over the chicken, seal the bag, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  3. When ready to cook, remove the chicken from the marinade and gently pat off excess liquid.
  4. Cook the chicken using one of the following methods:
    • Grill: Cook over medium heat until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
    • Bake: Bake at 400°F for 20 minutes, or until fully cooked.
    • Skillet: Cook over medium-high heat for 15–20 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through.
  5. Once cooked, allow the chicken to rest for 5 minutes, then dice or slice into thin strips.
  6. Divide the chicken evenly among 4 bowls. Top with diced red onion, cucumber, tomatoes, olives, feta cheese, and tzatziki sauce.
  7. Finish with a squeeze of fresh lemon juice. Toss gently before serving.
