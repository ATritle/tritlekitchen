Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients:

  • 2 - 12.5 oz canned chicken
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 3/4 cup buffalo sauce
  • 16 oz cream cheese
  • 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese

Directions:

    Instructions

    1. Drain the liquid from the canned chicken.
    2. Add all ingredients to a crockpot set to High. Stir occasionally until the cream cheese is fully melted and the mixture is heated through.
    3. Reduce heat to Warm and serve with tortilla chips.
