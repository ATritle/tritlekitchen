Better than Sex Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 box Devil's food cake
  • 7 oz can sweetened condensed milk
  • 6 oz caramel ice cream topping
  • 3 Heath bars - crushed
  • 8 oz container Cool Whip

Directions:

  1. Bake the cake according to package directions in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Allow the cake to cool completely, then use a fork to poke holes evenly across the surface.
  3. In a saucepan over low heat, combine the sweetened condensed milk and caramel. Stir until smooth and well blended.
  4. Slowly pour the caramel mixture evenly over the cake, allowing it to seep into the holes.
  5. Spread the whipped topping evenly over the cake. Drizzle with additional caramel and sprinkle with crushed Heath bars.
